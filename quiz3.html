Interactions and Impacts Questions

How do the Australian Privacy Principles affect the individual?
Australian Privacy Principles allow people to remain comfortable with the way their data is handled.
Under which legislation does the Australian Privacy Principles fall?
The Privacy Act, 1988.
Why is it important to de-identify personal data?
To protect the privacy of others. Leaving personal data in your results means someone else could learn info about your respondents.
What is an ethical dilemma in the context of data collection for research purposes?
An example is if you collect data that you need for research, but that data is also personal data that should not be released.
